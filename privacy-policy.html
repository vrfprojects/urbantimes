Privacy Policy for Flip and Ask

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

Overview

Flip and Ask ("the App") is a casual game designed for entertainment. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

Information Collection

  • No Personal Data Collected: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.
  • Local Storage: Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using standard storage mechanisms. This data is not shared with any third party.

Use of Information

The App uses local storage to save your game state and preferences. This information is only accessible on your device and is not transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

  • The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
  • No data is shared with external parties.

Permissions

The App may request basic permissions required for gameplay, such as internet access for rating the app on Google Play. No sensitive permissions (camera, contacts, etc.) are requested.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on the app's store listing.
