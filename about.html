About Urban Times

Who we are and why we do what we do.

Our Mission

Urban Times was founded with a simple goal: to tell the stories of cities and the people who make them. We believe that local journalism is the backbone of informed communities, and we are committed to delivering accurate, thoughtful, and engaging content.

What We Do

We cover a wide range of topics that affect city dwellers, including:

  • Urban planning and infrastructure
  • Local politics and community governance
  • Arts, culture, and entertainment
  • Business and economic development
  • Environment and sustainability

Our Team

Urban Times is powered by a passionate team of journalists, photographers, and editors who live and breathe city life. We are independently operated and committed to unbiased, community-first reporting.
