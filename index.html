About the game

Pass the phone.
Answer the question.

Flip and Ask is a pass-and-play card game for 2–6 players. Choose from multiple question decks — Fun, Deep, Funny, Couples, and more — and take turns flipping cards with thought-provoking or hilarious questions. No Wi-Fi needed. No accounts. Just great conversations.
